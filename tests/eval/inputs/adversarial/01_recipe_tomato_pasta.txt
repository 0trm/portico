Tomato Pasta

Ingredients:
- 400g spaghetti
- 800g canned San Marzano tomatoes
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- Salt
- Fresh basil

Instructions:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
2. While the pasta cooks, slice the garlic thin and warm it in the olive oil over low heat until fragrant but not brown.
3. Crush the tomatoes by hand into the pan, raise the heat to medium, and simmer for 12 minutes.
4. Drain the pasta, reserving 1/4 cup of cooking water. Toss pasta with sauce, adding cooking water as needed.
5. Tear basil over the top, drizzle with olive oil, and serve.
